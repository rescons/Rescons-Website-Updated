Some of Our Projects

( From Lab to Industry )

RESCONS Solutions Private Limited bridges the gap between research and real-world impact.

Have a glimpse of our game-changing technologies:

Gas Carburizing Software

Our cutting-edge software revolutionizes Gas Carburizing. An intuitive GUI allows real-time process control and offline optimization. Multiple industries have embraced this powerful tool to enhance their production efficiency and quality and achieve superior case hardening results in an environment-friendly way.

Silicon Carbide Production Technology

Our new technology boosted the production efficiency from 13% SiC (of the total charge) to a remarkable 32% SIC (of the total charge) of the global standard. Simultaneously, we achieved substantial energy savings. A major industry player has already entered a trial license agreement for this groundbreaking technology.

Boron Carbide Production

We have developed and modified the existing technology for the production of Boron Carbide, increasing production capacity while reducing boron loss and energy consumption. This technology is now available for transfer to industrial partners.

Rare Earth Extraction

Advanced extraction technologies focused on efficiency, sustainability, and strategic critical mineral development. Ready for industrial deployment.
