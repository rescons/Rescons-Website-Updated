Industrial-Scale Experiment of Biomass-Coal Co-Gasification to Produce Syngas for DRI Process

Industrial-scale co-gasification of biomass with coal was carried out in a long-term run at JSPL, Angul, Odisha. The produced syngas is effectively utilized in the DRI process, showcasing a sustainable and efficient approach to energy and steel production.
